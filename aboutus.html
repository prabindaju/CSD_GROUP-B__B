About Us

Hello and welcome to Doroco where every meal is a celebration of flavor, tradition, and family. Nestled in the heart of the city, Doroco is more than just a restaurant, it's a slice of Italy brought to your table.

OUR Story

Born from a passion for authentic Italian cuisine, Doroco was founded by the Rossi family, who brought generations of culinary secrets straight from the hills of Tuscany. Every dish we serve carries the warmth of our heritage, using recipes passed down with love and perfected over time.

From our family to yours

Whether you're here for a romantic dinner, a family celebration, or just craving a plate of real spaghetti alla carbonara, we welcome you with open arms and full plates. Mangia bene — eat well — and make yourself at home.
